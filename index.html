Welcome to Day Trading 101

Understanding the Basics

Day trading is when you buy and sell stuff in the same day. You have to be fast, focused, and careful or you can lose money quick

Most People trade stocks, forex, or futures trying to make money off of small price jumps

Key Points to Remember

  • Only use money you can afford to lose
  • Have a plan and stick to it
  • Keep learning and practicing
  • Use tools like charts and news to help you decide

Steps to Get Started

  1. Learn some trading strategies
  2. Open a trading account
  3. Practice on a fake/demo account
  4. Start small and be careful
