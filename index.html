Understanding the Basics

Day trading is when you buy and sell assets within the same day. You have to be fast, focused, and careful—or you can lose money quickly.

Most people trade stocks, forex, or futures, trying to make money off small price movements.

Key Points to Remember

  • Only use money you can afford to lose
  • Have a plan and stick to it
  • Keep learning and practicing
  • Use tools like charts and news to help you decide

Steps to Get Started

  1. Learn some trading strategies
  2. Open a trading account
  3. Practice on a demo account
  4. Start small and be careful
